# FireComply — The Elevator Pitch

## 30-Second Version (MSP)

> "FireComply gives MSPs a single pane of glass for Sophos firewall security posture across their entire customer base. Connect Sophos Central and Connector Agents to automatically track every firewall's configuration, score it against 37 best-practice checks and 39 compliance frameworks, detect drift, and deliver branded reports — all from one workspace. Your customers get their own branded portal. You get a scalable, repeatable security assessment service you can bill for."

## 30-Second Version (SE)

> "FireComply turns raw Sophos firewall configuration exports into audit-grade security findings, compliance-mapped evidence, and client-ready reports — in minutes, not days. Upload a config, get a deterministic posture score against 37 Sophos best-practice checks and 39 compliance frameworks, then generate AI-powered technical, executive, and compliance reports with one click. No agent required — but if you want continuous monitoring, a lightweight connector automates the whole thing on a schedule."

## 10-Second Version

> "Upload a Sophos firewall config. Get a best-practice score, compliance mapping, and professional reports — in two minutes. Scale it across hundreds of customers with automated collection, drift detection, and branded portals."

## For MSP Partners (Business Value)

> "FireComply gives your team a repeatable, scalable way to assess every customer's firewall posture, track drift over time, and deliver branded compliance reports — without hiring a dedicated security analyst. It turns firewall management from a cost centre into a revenue stream: charge for assessments, bill for ongoing compliance monitoring, and use the data to identify upsell opportunities."

## For SEs (Internal)

> "FireComply is your health-check-in-a-box. Request a customer's config via a secure link, get it back pre-analysed, walk them through the score on a call, and email the report before the call ends. No manual analysis. No spreadsheets."

## For Customers (What They See)

> "Your MSP provides you with a branded security portal showing your firewall's posture score, findings, compliance alignment, and downloadable reports — always current, always accessible."
