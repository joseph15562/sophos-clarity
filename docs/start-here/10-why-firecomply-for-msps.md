# Why FireComply for MSPs

## The Problem Today

As an MSP, firewall management across your customer base is a constant juggling act:

- **No single pane of glass** — You log into each customer's Sophos Central tenant individually. There's no unified view of firewall posture across your entire portfolio.
- **Health checks don't scale** — A thorough firewall review takes 1–3 hours per device. With 50+ customer firewalls, you'd need a full-time resource just to cycle through them annually.
- **Compliance is reactive** — When a customer asks "are we Cyber Essentials compliant?", you either spend days auditing or give a best-guess answer. Neither is acceptable.
- **Drift goes unnoticed** — A customer's IT team changes a rule at 2am. Nobody knows until something breaks — or an auditor finds it.
- **Reporting is manual** — You build reports in Word from scratch. Every customer gets a slightly different format. There's no repeatable template, no library, no archive.
- **Customer communication is ad-hoc** — You email PDFs and hope they read them. There's no self-service portal where customers can check their own posture.

## What FireComply Changes

### Before: Fragmented, Manual, Reactive

1. Log into each customer's Central tenant separately
2. Download config export from each firewall
3. Manually review configs against a mental checklist
4. Write up findings in Word
5. Email to customer, hope they act on it
6. Repeat quarterly — or more realistically, annually

### After: Unified, Automated, Proactive

1. **Fleet Command** shows every firewall across every customer on one screen — with scores, grades, status, and a world map
2. **Connector Agents** automatically pull configs on a schedule — no manual exports
3. **Drift Monitor** alerts you when configs change between scans
4. **AI reports** generate Technical Handover, Executive Summary, and Compliance reports in seconds
5. **Client Portals** give customers branded self-service access to their posture, findings, and reports
6. **Mission Control** gives you a real-time dashboard of portfolio risk, alerts, and activity

### Specific MSP Benefits

**Portfolio visibility** — Mission Control and Fleet Command give you a single view of every customer's firewall posture. See who's at risk, who's overdue for a review, and where your attention is needed most.

**Scalable assessments** — Connector Agents automate config collection. Scheduled reports auto-generate and deliver to customers on a cadence. A 200-firewall estate takes the same effort as 2.

**Revenue-generating compliance** — The Compliance Readiness Report maps firewall configuration against 39 frameworks (GDPR, Cyber Essentials, NCSC, ISO 27001, PCI DSS, HIPAA, NIST, etc.) with control-by-control evidence tables. This is a deliverable you can bill for.

**Customer retention** — Client Portals with your branding (logo, colours, SLA info) give customers a professional, always-current view of their security posture. QBR materials write themselves.

**Drift detection** — The Drift Monitor compares config snapshots over time and flags what changed. You know about rule changes before they become incidents.

**PSA integration** — ConnectWise Manage, Datto Autotask, and ConnectWise Cloud integrations mean assessment findings flow into your existing ticketing and billing workflows.

**Team collaboration** — Workspace members (admins and viewers) share the same customer library, report archive, and fleet view. No more "which spreadsheet has the latest?"

## The Commercial Angle

FireComply turns firewall management from a cost centre into a revenue stream:

1. **Security Assessment as a Service** — Charge for the initial assessment (Technical Handover + Executive Summary). The report quality justifies the fee.
2. **Ongoing Compliance Monitoring** — Monthly/quarterly retainer for continuous posture monitoring via Connector Agents + scheduled reports.
3. **QBR materials in one click** — Client Portals and saved reports give you everything you need for a Quarterly Business Review without any prep.
4. **Upsell identification** — The deterministic analysis flags specific Sophos product gaps (no DPI, no web filtering, no MDR) — each is a conversation about additional licences.
5. **Compliance evidence** — The Compliance Readiness Report is a deliverable that compliance officers, auditors, and board members pay attention to.
